How to Play

Tap tiles to earn cash, buy upgrades to earn faster, and build your city over time.

  • Tap a City Tile to earn money.
  • Tiles can level up randomly as you tap.
  • Tap Boost increases earnings per tap.
  • Passive Income earns money every second.
  • City Development boosts all tile bonuses.
  • Export/Import to backup your save.
  • Offline earnings can appear when you return.
